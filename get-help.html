What to Include When Asking for Help

1
Clear Description

Explain what you're trying to achieve and what's not working. Avoid vague statements like "it doesn't work."

2
Error Messages

Copy the exact error message. Don't paraphrase. Screenshots help if the error is visual.

3
Code Snippets

Share relevant code. Use code blocks or GitHub gists. Don't send entire projects via email.

4
What You've Tried

List the solutions you've already attempted. This helps avoid suggesting things you've already tried.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond within 24 hours on weekdays. Weekend responses may take 48 hours. For urgent issues, include "[URGENT]" in your email subject.
Can you help with my college project?
Yes! We help students with academic projects. We provide guidance and suggestions but don't do the work for you. Share your project requirements and we'll help you get started.
Do you provide live coding sessions?
We can schedule short screen-sharing sessions for complex issues after initial email discussion. These are typically 15-30 minutes focused on specific problems.
What if my question is very basic?
No question is too basic! We're here to help learners at all levels. However, try searching online first - you might find instant solutions on Stack Overflow or documentation.
Can I share large files or codebases?
Use GitHub/GitLab for code. For other files, use Google Drive or WeTransfer and share the link. Don't attach large files directly to emails.
