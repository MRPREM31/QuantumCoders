Smart Attendance System is a digital attendance management application designed for educational institutions.

GPS & Location Features

Understanding location-based features and your control over them

Location Validation

Approximate device location checked during attendance marking

Proxy Prevention

Prevents attendance marking from unauthorized locations

Optional Feature

Location access can be disabled based on institutional settings

No Continuous Tracking

We do not track users continuously or store location history

Privacy First: Location features are designed with privacy in mind. When enabled, location is checked only at the moment of attendance marking and is not stored or tracked beyond that moment.
