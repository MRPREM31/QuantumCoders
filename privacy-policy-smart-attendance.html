Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Attendance App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information to provide secure and reliable attendance management.

Effective Date:
Last Updated:
Version: 1.0

About This Policy

Smart Attendance App ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and maintained by QuantumCoders Tech Lab.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Important: By using Smart Attendance App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to ensure proper functioning of the Smart Attendance App:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • User Role (Student/Teacher)
  • Academic Details

Device Information

  • Device ID (Android ID)
  • Device Model & OS Version
  • App Version Information

Location Information

  • GPS Location during attendance
  • Classroom verification data
  • One-time location check

Attendance Data

  • Attendance Status
  • Date & Time Stamps
  • Subject & Class Details
  • Teacher Information

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • User Authentication: To verify student and teacher identities securely
  • Attendance Verification: To confirm classroom presence using geo-fencing technology
  • Proxy Prevention: To prevent duplicate or fake attendance submissions
  • Record Management: To generate accurate attendance records and reports
  • Session Management: To allow teachers to create and manage attendance sessions
  • App Improvement: To enhance performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Security Enhancement: To implement security measures and prevent unauthorized access

3. Location Data Usage

Smart Attendance App uses location data strictly for attendance verification purposes:

Important Location Policy

  • Location is accessed only when scanning the attendance QR code
  • Location is NOT tracked continuously or in the background
  • Location data is NOT stored for tracking or monitoring purposes
  • Location access is mandatory to prevent remote or proxy attendance
  • Location data is used solely for classroom presence verification

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure Cloud Storage: All data is stored in encrypted cloud databases
  • Access Control: Strict access restrictions for authorized personnel only
  • Data Encryption: All data transmissions use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Compliance: Adherence to data protection regulations and best practices
  • Backup Systems: Regular data backups and disaster recovery protocols

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to support app functionality:

Firebase Services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore Database
  • Firebase Hosting

Google Cloud

  • Google Sheets Integration
  • Apps Script Automation
  • Cloud Functions

Important: These services are used exclusively for app functionality. None of these services use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the features you use in Smart Attendance App.

6. Data Sharing Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy:

  • No Data Selling: We do NOT sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties
  • Limited Sharing: Data is shared only when required for core app functionality
  • Authorized Access: Attendance data is visible only to authorized teachers and administrators
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or legal process
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for analytics and improvement

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Right to Access

View your personal and attendance data at any time through the app interface.

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data by contacting us.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below.

Right to Object

Opt-out of certain data collection (may affect app functionality).

8. Children's Privacy

Special considerations for younger users:

  • Smart Attendance App is designed for educational institutions and students
  • We do NOT knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age
  • If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately
  • Parental consent is recommended for users under 18 years old
  • Educational institutions are responsible for obtaining necessary consents

9. App Permissions Explained

Smart Attendance App requires the following permissions:

Camera To scan QR codes for attendance
Location To verify classroom presence
Internet To sync attendance data securely
Storage To download attendance reports

Note: All permissions are used exclusively for their intended purposes as described above. We do not access any features beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically:

  • Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date
  • Significant changes will be notified through the app interface
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance
  • Previous versions of this policy are available upon request
  • We encourage periodic review of this policy for updates
